Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Acre Grove is committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information you provide to us when you use our website or submit a land assessment request.

1. Who We Are

Acre Grove is a land acquisition consultancy operating across Manchester, Derby, the North West and East Midlands. We operate in joint venture with Land Profits (land-profits.co.uk).

Contact: enquire@acregrove.com  ·  07889 024 897

2. What Data We Collect

When you submit a land assessment request, we collect:

  • Your name, email address, and phone number
  • Information about your land (location, type, size, planning history)
  • Any additional information you choose to provide

We do not collect any data automatically through cookies or tracking technologies on this website.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to:

  • Respond to your land assessment request
  • Assess the development potential of your land
  • Contact you with relevant information about our services

We will never use your data for unsolicited marketing or pass it to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data on the basis of your explicit consent, given when you tick the consent checkbox on our contact form. You may withdraw this consent at any time by contacting us at info@simpsonandsonestates.com.

5. Data Sharing

Your personal data will not be sold, rented, or shared with any third party without your explicit consent, except where required by law.

Where we work in joint venture with Land Profits on your land, we may share relevant land details with them as part of the assessment process. You will always be informed before this happens.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as is necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by law. If you withdraw consent or request deletion, we will remove your data within 30 days.

7. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the ICO (ico.org.uk)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at enquire@acregrove.com.

8. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
